About

With the motto of keeping the beauty industry within the reach of all sorts of women and engaging educated women in the industry, Lawyer Noor E Jannat started her dream project Allure Beauty Parlour on May 27th, 2018. Along with fair pricing, she ensured the quality and products. Also, Allure Beauty Parlour is one of the few Parlour in Bangladesh where clients can share their experience and reviews directly with the owner. Within one year Allure Beauty Parlour became one of the best Beauty Parlour in town.
